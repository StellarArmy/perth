What is tricycle?

We are looking for Kepler eclipsing binaries with starspot signals from both stars. That's three periods: one from the eclipses (telling us about masses and radii), and two from the starspot signals (telling us about rotation periods and magnetic activity). As a population, such systems would provide benchmarks for things like stellar evolution models and gyrochronology.
